Welcome to Mount Barker Olive Grove

Nestled in the heart of Mount Barker, Wanaka, New Zealand, our olives thrive in the stunning landscape, unique climate and our olive oil captures the essence of this remarkable place in every drop.

THE GROVE

Born from this land, our boutique grove of mixed Italian, Spanish and French olives prospers in the pristine environment. Our olives are grown in a natural, spray free environment, yielding an olive oil that is rich in flavour, pure in nature and full of the essence of the land.

EXPLORE THE GROVE

THE OIL

Hand harvested and crafted carefully from this mixed variety grove comes a blended oil of a truly distinguished flavour. The unique terroir of Mount Barker, with its cool climate and long summer days, gives our olives a colour and flavour profile that sets our award winning oil apart.

THE SPACE

The grove is situated at 609 Mount Barker Road. North facing and high on the hill, the views are truly breathtaking. On established grounds, the magnificent five bedroom home with its turret and fairy tale surrounds sits atop of the boutique olive grove.

THE STORY

The Prince/Baker family first took ownership of the Mount Barker property in 2013, inheriting a young but established olive grove, the family have carefully tended and cultivated the trees increasing the olive bounty year on year.
